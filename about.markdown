My name is Andreas Jonasson, and I see myself as a perpetual student. I do not see myself as a perpetual student per the definition of [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Perpetual_student). Instead, I want to see myself as a person always striving to learn and deepen my knowledge.

I finished my Degree of Master of Science in Engineering: Game and Software Engineering at [Blekinge Institute of Technology](https://www.bth.se/eng/) in 2018. Ever since I took my master I have been working in higher education. I started working at Blekinge Institute of Technology the early summer of 2018 and stayed there to the end of 2019. As of January 2020, I work as an adjunct at the Divison of Game Development at the [University of Skövde](https://his.se/en/). 

# Why I created this site
One thing I find important in my role as a teacher is the continuing development of my own knowledge and understanding. This development is not only connected to the area(s) I am teaching, as I also believe it is important that I develop my pedagogical understanding. 

Therefore this site was created with a simple goal in mind; documenting the studies I conduct on my own free time. This documentation serves three distinct purposes:
1. By making it a habbit of documenting the process I aim to put a larger emphasis on understanding the process and subject
2. By documenting the process I'm able to go back and reflect on my understanding and the journey I have taken
3. By making it a habbit of writing these documentations I hope to get a bit more motivation to keep up my studies
